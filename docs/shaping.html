Shaping

After analyzing and exploring the details of the area, the formation of the building could start. To perform calculations, we needed to establish an initial shape for the building, allowing the computer to then generate further iterations. We opted to shape the initial building envelope using voxels measuring 3.6 by 3.6 by 3 meters. This dimension aligns with a standard grid size of 7.2 meters. The building's form emerged by considering several characteristics of the area that we aimed to incorporate into the final design. For instance, it was crucial for us that the building maintained a distance from the structures behind it, creating an open street for the entrances of other buildings. We also took into account the yellow route as a guiding principle.

A pivotal starting point in our process is the REL Chart that we developed. In this chart, we weigh all significant functions against each other, influencing the final calculation based partly on our subjective perspective regarding the distribution of functions within the building. This chart played a crucial role in the eventual region growing phase of the process.
